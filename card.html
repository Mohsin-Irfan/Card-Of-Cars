The new Honda Civic will be offered in three variants. The Base and mid-level variants will have a turbocharged 1.5-liter four-cylinder petrol engine that produces 127 horsepower (hp) and 180 Newton-meters (Nm) of torque and is mated to an M-CVT automatic transmission.

Toyota Corolla Altis Grande 2022 Review

The Corolla Altis Grande 2022 has the option of having an all-black interior. The front and rear seats in the Altis 1.8L CVTi are ivory fabric, while those in the Altis Grande are leather. The base variant comes standard with air conditioning, an infotainment system, power steering, an Eco meter, and a tachometer.
